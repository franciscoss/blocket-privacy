Blocket Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-12-13

Data Collection

Blocket does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All information is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API.

Data Storage

All data, including your list of blocked websites and time limits, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your computer and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Permissions

Blocket requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your blocked sites configuration locally
  • Tabs: To manage navigation and redirect to interstitial pages
  • Web Navigation: To intercept navigation to blocked sites
  • Host Permissions: To check if any website matches your blocked sites list

All processing is done locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Blocket does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
